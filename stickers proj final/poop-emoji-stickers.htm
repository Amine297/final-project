Description:

Poop Emoji Stickers…. “WHHHHHYYYYYY?” You ask? “But we’ll… Why not?!?!!” Is what my reply would be. 

If you could witness as we have, the number of times we have seen kids and adults start to bust out loud and deep belly laughs, silly jokes, faces and stories you’ll know exactly why we stock this range, and a whole range of poop emoji items. It’s amazing to watch people light up and see the shame fade, and humour step into otherwise serious matters wrapped up with all sorts of emotions. We love to help unpack those with our range of poop emoji items, and hope that you will love them just as much as we do (and our humour and all the puns to go along with them also!) 

Seriously though… Life is too short to not have some fun, to not laugh, and to not make light of some otherwise serious conversations and matters of the gut! When you have battled the Embarrassement, fear, shame and stigma of bowel conditions - you will do anything to help reduce that same shame and stigma for others — BECAUSE THERE IS NO SHAME in battling bowel or poop issues, + it’s not your fault. 

It’s also high time we lifted the lid (literally) on this unspoken subject and brought some life, light, and a sense of humour to otherwise often serious matters.


Note: We acknowledge not everyone has the same humour and not everyone will see this as funny. If this isn’t for you, that’s totally ok! We have lots of other items that we hope you’ll enjoy on our site as well!
